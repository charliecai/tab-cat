Tab Cat Icon Concepts

Six directions for a Chrome Web Store icon. The selected direction keeps the cat silhouette strong at 16px while still showing the tab-management metaphor at larger sizes.

Brand: Tab Cat · Category: Browser productivity

Stacked Tab Cat

Best small-size readability. The top layer reads as grouped tabs, while the black cat face stays recognizable in Chrome toolbar and store search results.

Orbit Tabs

A compact mascot-first mark with tab lines behind the face. Stronger as an app icon than as a detailed product metaphor.

Domain Grid

Emphasizes grouped domains and dashboard layout. Useful for product pages, but the cat becomes secondary at small sizes.

Reading Save

Connects directly to Save for later and Reading inbox. The bookmark metaphor is clear but less representative of open-tab cleanup.

Whisker Tabs

A warmer mascot direction. Friendly and memorable, but the horizontal whiskers can become thin in the 16px extension icon.

Minimal Tab Mark

The most conservative SaaS-style option. It is tidy and scalable, though it loses some of the distinct brand warmth.
